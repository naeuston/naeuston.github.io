Clarification

There are a great number of addiction-related issues that others might expect a worldwide society of recovering drug addicts to take a postion on. Our answer, according to Tradition 10, is that our groups and our fellowship take no position, pro or con, on any issues except the NA programme itself. For our own survival, we have no opinion on outside issues.
