This is the website for "Euston... We have a problem" - a weekly meeting of Narcotics Anonymous in Euston, London. For more information on Narcotics Anonymous and to find other meetings please visit ukna.org.

Important Notice

In light of the COVID-19 pandemic, the Euston meeting has switched to an online-only format using Zoom.

Please do not go to our old location near Euston station - this location is closed for the foreseeable future.


When

Every Sunday at 11:00am London time (1hr 30m)

Where to find us

You can join our meetings at https://zoom.us/j/271542518. Password: 1234

How to join us

To join our Zoom meeting, click the above link, enter your name
For anonymity we recommend you do not use your full name) and join.

You do not have to sign up or give your email address. It is the conscience of the group that you must enable your PC webcam or mobile phone camera for the duration of the meeting. This is for the safety and protection of the group.

Zoom virtual meeting will work on all PC/Mac computers.

On your mobile phones and tablets you will need to install the Zoom app. It can be download on the Apple App Store for iPad/iPhone and the Google Play Store for Android phones.

If this is your first time joining our virtual meeting, you may wish to give yourself extra time to install it and familiarise yourself with its operation.

Meeting format

This is a step meeting, alternating between a step reading and step share each week. If there is a fifth Sunday in the month, there is a topic share.

It is a closed meeting for addicts only but can be opened for non-addicts upon request. Non-addicts are asked not to share.

Sharing time is limited to 3 minutes and please be aware this is a large meeting so not everyone may get a chance to share.
