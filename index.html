Meeting format

This is a step meeting, alternating between a step reading and step share each week. There will be a topic share if there is a fifth Sunday in that month.

Closed meeting for addicts only. Can be made opened for non-addicts upon request. However, non-addicts may not share. There is a timer for shares.

Other meeting info

Children welcome. No animals, no smoking by decree of landlord. Not wheelchair accessible
