This is the website for the "Euston... We have a problem." meeting of Narcotics Anonymous.

For more about Narcotics Anonymous and to find other meetings take a look at the UK NA website

Important Notice

In light of the current health situation (COVID-19 pandemic) the Euston meeting has switched to an online-only format with immediate effect. Please do not go to our old location near Euston station - this location is closed for the foreseeable future.


When

Every Sunday at 11:00am GMT (1 hour and 45 mins)

Where to find us

Our virtual meeting is located at https://zoom.us/j/271542518.
When prompted, use the password "1234".
Our physical meeting is closed for the foreseeable future.

How to join us

To join our virtual meeting hosted on the Zoom platform, click the above link, join the room and enter your first name.


You do not have to sign up or give your email address. You will need to enable your webcam / phone camera while on the call for the safety of the group.


Zoom virtual meeting will work on all PC/Mac computers. On your phone and tablets you will need to install the Zoom app. It can be download on the Apple App Store and the Google Play Store.


If this is your first time joining our virtual meeting, you may wish to give yourself extra time to install it on your phone or tablet and familiarise yourself with its features.

Meeting format

Online-only until further notice. This is a step meeting, alternating between a step reading and step share each week. There will be a topic share if there is a fifth Sunday in that month.

Closed meeting for addicts only. Can be made opened for non-addicts upon request. However, non-addicts are asked not to share. There is a timer for shares.

Other meeting info

We ask you to please have your basic text with you in case you get picked to do a reading.
